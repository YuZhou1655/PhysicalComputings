Ford Mustang Boss 428 Cardboard ver.

The prompt of first Physical Computing is "Something about You". Boss 429 is one of the most classic and highly valued Ford Mustang, and Ford Mustang is always my favorite model ever since I knew it exists when I was 11 years old. Naturally, I took the chance to build one of my own cardboard version of Ford Mustang Boss 429 made with with arduino boards, cricut design and python.

Weather Box

This time we are going to make something related to "Life Hack" and it could be useful or useless. This weather box is designed to help you indentify the weather and provide some emergency light in dark. It comes with a light sensor, on the one hand, it will detects the weather based the light it received, on the other hand, it will turns the light on itself when the enviroment gets too dark.
